Scaffold ≠ stability

Per-pair token alignment: final-answer markers land at x=0. Negative x = scaffold tokens, positive x = answer tokens. Drafting headings are treated as scaffold, not answers. Many reasoning outputs never reach a final answer within 512 tokens, so answer-side reasoning curves only appear when enough final-answer pairs survive. The raw token-index stability mostly reflected scaffold template structure, not clean answer-content robustness.
