A practical split emerged on tooling: many strongly recommend using Scheme (via MIT Scheme or Racket with the sicp package) over the JavaScript edition, arguing Scheme’s minimal syntax makes the ideas pop, while others warn that MIT Scheme can be finicky on modern machines but DrRacket works fine.
