SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This page covers SMS messages sent by David Rose's personal automation tools for agent approval requests. These messages are used to ask for a simple yes/no approval before a personal automation task continues.

Information Collected

The SMS approval system may process your mobile phone number, message delivery metadata, and the content of replies such as START, HELP, STOP, YES, or NO. The system does not collect payment information, health data, government identifiers, or marketing profiles through SMS.

How Information Is Used

Mobile numbers and SMS replies are used only to send, receive, and audit personal automation approval messages. Message frequency varies and depends on when an automation needs approval.

Mobile Number Sharing

Mobile phone numbers collected for this SMS program are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. SMS data may be processed by service providers such as Twilio solely to deliver and operate the messaging service.

Opt-Out and Help

Reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages. Reply HELP for help. After opting out, you may receive one final confirmation message. Message and data rates may apply.

Retention

SMS request and response logs may be retained for operational debugging, abuse prevention, and audit history, then deleted when no longer needed.

Contact

Questions about this SMS program can be sent to david@drose.io.
