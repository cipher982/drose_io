SMS Terms

Last updated: June 1, 2026

By opting in to David Rose's personal automation SMS approval messages, you agree to receive text messages asking for yes/no approval before a personal automation task continues.

Program Description

Messages are sent for personal agent approval workflows, such as confirming whether an automation may proceed with a specific requested action. Messages are informational and transactional, not promotional.

Opt-In

You can opt in by texting START to +1 205-651-6153 or by directly asking David Rose to enable SMS approval messages for your mobile number. A confirmation message may be sent after opt-in.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Messages are sent only when an automation needs approval or when you request help, start, or stop instructions.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Opt-Out

You can cancel SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. After replying STOP, you may receive one confirmation text and then no further messages unless you opt in again.
